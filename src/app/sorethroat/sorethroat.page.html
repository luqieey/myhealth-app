Sore throat

Causes Most sore throats happen because you have a viral infection, like the common cold or flu. Sore throats may be a symptom of the following conditions or issues:

Allergies,Tonsillitis, Acid reflux, Bacterial infection,Overuse or irritants

Symptoms Apart from throat pain, sore throat symptoms may include:

Fever,headache,Nasal congestion (stuffy nose),Swollen lymph nodes. Runny nose, Cough.


Treatment Sucking on throat lozenges or hard candies may ease sore throat symptoms. Other suggestions include:
1) Drink warm liquids
2) Sucking on ice chips or popsicles may help sore throat pain.
3) If your throat is sore from shouting, screaming, singing or even talking a lot, resting your voice may help.
4) Avoid irritants

2) Avoid people who are sneezing and coughing.
3) If you do spend time with people who are sneezing and coughing, avoid sharing food, drink or utensils.
